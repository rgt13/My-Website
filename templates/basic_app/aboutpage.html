About Me

My name is Ryan Thomas. I was born in the great city of Nashville, Tennessee on February 19, 1995. I currently reside in the small town of Naples, FL. I am an avid supporter of the Tennessee Titans, a stout advocate of Bayern Munich Football Club, and a very motivated software developer. I strive to be a professional Software Engineer, constantly learning new methodologies, frameworks, and styles of coding.

I am a proud graduate of Florida State University earning a B.S. in Computer Engineering, as well as a minor in Mathematics. I am a member of the world class organization of Theta Chi Fraternity, where I've dedicated countless hours providing for new members, participating in numerous philanthropies, courting for homecoming, and winning multiple intramural sports championships. Go Noles!

My interest in programming is mainly a consequence of my love for video games. I've played just about every popular video game that's ever been released, and I still play plenty to this day. I've actually programmed one of my own as part of a group project in college, completely through Unity and C#. The details can be found on the projects page of this website. Click the button below to play the game!

My main coding language proficiencies include C, C++, C#, Java, Python, Ruby, and SQL for the backend. For the frontend, they consist of Javascript(Node.js), HTML, CSS, jQuery, AJAX, and Bootstrap/SemanticUI styling. I also have extensive experience in API and server design, through the use of Flask, Django, MySQL, PostGreSQL, Rails, Apache Tomcat, and Java Servlets. I've gained experience in unit, system and integration tests using PyTest, PyUnit, DocTest & Nose with Python, jUnit with Java, and MS Tests with C#.
